About Corrosion Protection Council of India

The Corrosion Protection Council of India (CPCI) has been established with the goal of developing and implementing innovative strategies and technologies to achieve corrosion-free construction practices. This mission is critical for enhancing the safety, sustainability, and long-term resilience of infrastructure, and for contributing to the overall well-being of communities across India.

Corrosion has long posed a significant threat to India’s infrastructure and economic stability, silently consuming a substantial portion of the country’s GDP. The formation of CPCI marks a pivotal step in addressing this issue through coordinated and strategic action. Recognizing the pressing need for a national effort, the council brings together experts and leaders from across industries to proactively combat the corrosive impact on vital infrastructure.

CPCI is committed to fostering collaboration between government bodies, industry stakeholders, and academic institutions to drive innovation in corrosion protection. The council aims to lead policy reforms, promote advanced technologies, and raise public awareness about the severe risks posed by corrosion to infrastructure longevity and public safety.

One of CPCI's key approaches involves direct engagement with contractors, engineers, consultants, authorities, and academia to spread awareness and build technical capacity on corrosion prevention practices. This hands-on engagement will support the widespread adoption of durable and effective corrosion protection systems, particularly in vulnerable regions like India’s extensive coastal zones.

Through collective expertise and focused initiatives, CPCI is dedicated to mitigating the economic drain caused by corrosion and ensuring that India’s infrastructure is robust, sustainable, and future-ready.
