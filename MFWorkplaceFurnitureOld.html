These screens are free standing and self-supporting and have overall thickness of 28mm. These comprise of an external framework comprising of vertical and horizontal members made from extruded aluminium sections duly powder coated. These vertical channels have grooves in them to accept a slider nut mechanism for mounting all panel-mounted accessories.

The joinery system of these screens is simple and effective in order to install the panels in any desired configuration and also to dismantle and reinstall in any other altered configuration. Two adjacent screens are connected to each other by means of PVC extrusions.

Screens are connected to each other in a “T” “L” “+” and triangular configuration by means of round aluminium extrusions duly powder coated.
