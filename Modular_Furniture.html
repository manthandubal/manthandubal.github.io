Business

MODULAR FURNITURE

EPL Modular is a leader in design and innovation for the modular furniture market, with a portfolio including some of the nation’s most significant and complex government and corporate projects. It is a socially responsible company; manufacturing eco-friendly products which meet the needs of the client while at the same time sustain the environment.

Our Factory is located in 34 acre complex, having a covered area of over 6000 sq. meters. Based on Flexible Manufacturing Systems, we can meet all design specifications of its clients and architects and to give our clients the best, the plants technology is constantly upgraded. With our 27 years vast experience, we assure quality and accuracy to our valuable customers. We have successfully completed and handed over more than 20000 sites and have covered around 200 lakhs square feet area across India.
