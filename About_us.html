About Eurocoustic Products Ltd

With over 27 years of multifaceted track record under its belt, the Eurocoustic Products Group (EPL) expanded and then diversified from modular furniture and anti-corrosive coatings into five verticals that support and service the booming Indian infrastructure sector:

  • Modular Office Furniture
  • Fusion-Bonded Epoxy Coating
  • Fabrication of steel Structurals, Buildings and Bridges
  • Corrugated Steel-Arch Bridges
  • Bio Fuels

EPL offers its clients two of the greatest advantages crucial to successful execution of mega projects in the infrastructure sector:

  1.  It offers Design, Fabrication, and Erection under one umbrella, thereby eliminating time-sapping, multiple-agency coordination.

  2.  Its ability to deliver and commission in a timely manner, results in on-time completion of projects for its clients.

  • Message from Chairman’s Desk

At Eurocoustic Products Ltd, we believe that success comes from a relentless focus on shared vision, innovation and execution.

With a modest beginning in Modular Furniture, we evolved rapidly and diversified into Fusion Bonded Epoxy Coating, Steel Structures, Buildings, Bridges and our most recent venture, Biofuel - all in a span of 27 years.

On the one hand, our diversity and, on the other hand, our Group’s entrepreneurial spirit has led to its consistent success over the years.

Service, Quality and Safety are the cornerstones of our growth, and that alone sets us apart. We pursue the highest qualities for our products, services and operations to attain the highest levels of customer satisfaction and social value.

And of course, our most valuable asset is our human resources. We nurture the careers of the employees while depending on their skills and potential. Our goal is to make sure our employees have opportunities to prosper and succeed.

We seek to maximize the corporate values and profits while trying to meet the expectations of our customers, suppliers, communities and employees, for our success would not be possible without the loyalty of our customers and employees who have shared in our incredible journey.

We look forward to innovating, executing and identifying good entrepreneurial skills while focusing on making our workplace attractive, supportive and inclusive.

To become India’s leading Structural Steel solution and sustainable energy provider.

To fuel economic growth in India in the infra and energy sector by providing cost effective, innovative, and sustainable solutions to our clients.
