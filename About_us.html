About Eurocoustic Products Ltd

With more than two and half decades of multifaceted experience, “EPL GROUP” has grown to carve a singular unique niche for themselves in the field of Furniture, Coatings, Energy, Steel and Infrastructure. EPL constantly learns, upgrades and invests in technology, equipment and human resource to remain a step ahead of tomorrow’s industrial needs, inadvertently being well positioned to meet the future needs of Global Infrastructure.

Today EPL is geared to offer diverse products and services throughout the country.

EPL has set up business facilities in various core sectors of India, with verticals that are exploring innovative and sustainable avenues in modular furniture, Epoxy Coating, Fabrication of Steel Structures, Corrugated Steel Arch Bridges, Bio-Fuel and Bio-coal. EPL is also leading in every sector that it operates in.

We have achieved excellent brand equity by maintaining a disciplined focus on excellence in quality, delivery on time and value for money for our clients.
