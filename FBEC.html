We would like to introduce ourself as leader in coating application technology and has automated solutions for applying Fusion Bonded Epoxy Coating (FBEC) to all sizes of rebars.

Inspired to meet the growing demand for long lasting infrastructure in India as well as in the region in general, Eurocoustic Products Limited has embarked upon a series of initiatives aimed at increasing its production capacity. State-of-the-art technically advanced expansion projects are designed to produce world class products. Over the years, Eurocoustic Products Limited has successfully forged and reliability in all its coatings and service offerings. Central to this achievement has been the drive to exceed customer’s expectations.

We have been providing epoxy coating on reinforcement bars of projects of National importance which include Metros, Power Sector, Bridges, Nuclear complexes and several others.

FBEC Process Description

Fusion-bonded epoxy coatings for rebars are not applied on the job. Instead, the bars are coated in a strictly controlled factory process where epoxy powders are fusion-bonded to the steel surface. The powder is applied electrostatically to the cleaned surface of a hot steel rebar, where it fuses, melts down and cures to an adherent, continuous and chemically cross-linked protective film. This coating significantly reduces the amount of current required for cathodic protection to prevent corrosion. FBE Coating has been laboratory and field tested to assure customers of field-proven protection that will last for generations.
